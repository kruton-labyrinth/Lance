About:

Lance is all about compassion and care for animals, specifically stray ones without shelter or anywhere to go, many starve to death, and we produce high quality products at minimal prices to help the ones in need but couldn't help themselves. Come join us on our mission of helping these poor animals.

Story:

I'm Saqeeb Chand the founder of Lance, as a young teen I was astonished when I found out that each year approximately 2.7 million stray animals die due to starvation. We might give food to them once in a while which lasts them a day but what about the others in a week? The Stray Feeder I came up with was to solve this very problem, inspired by the Mamamatik Project in Turkey
